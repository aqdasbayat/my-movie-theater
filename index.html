Leo is a fun and heartwarming animated movie that follows the story of a grumpy but lovable lizard named Leo who has spent decades in a school classroom as a class pet. But his plans take a wild turn as he ends up helping students with their problems in unexpected and hilarious ways. Full of laughs, life lessons, and sweet moments, Leo is a great family film with a lot of heart.
